Lasagna Recipe

Description

A Homemade Lasagna for everyone to make it in a rainy Sunday, or even at night in a special occasion with the loved ones.

Lasagna made with meat and cheese to brighten your holy moment of eating a meal with a good company.

Ingredients

  • Meat(Of your choice)
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Seasoning
  • Eggs
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the tomato sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cheese, eggs, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
